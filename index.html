Week 4 — Social Listening

Facebook Groups to Join

Brett, join these 15 groups using your personal Facebook account. Tick each one off as you go. Spend a few days reading each group's tone and rules before engaging. Don't sell anything — just be the helpful local solar guy.

Tier 1

Solar & Energy-Specific Groups

Highest intent. People here are actively researching, comparing quotes, or troubleshooting. This is where the leads are.

Tier 2

Home Renovation & Building Groups

Solar decisions happen during renos and new builds. People here are spending money on their homes.

Tier 3

Broad Brisbane Community Groups

High reach. These are the "anyone know a good..." groups. Value is name recognition over time.

Tier 4

Suburb-Specific Groups

Smaller but hyper-local trust. Neighbours recommending neighbours carries the most weight.

After joining all 15, spend a few days just reading each group. Get a feel for the tone, the rules about business posts, and what kind of questions people ask. Then we'll set up monitoring to flag the good ones for you to reply to.
